The Beauty Of Virginity

Foreword

I must confess without mincing words that the book “Beauty of Virginity” is a must read for everyone taking the journey towards adulthood. The book cut across all areas you would need answers to as a teenager or youth in five (5) solid chapters that would most definitely eradicate the moral decadence in our society.

The area of social-pressure, loneliness, music-videos, magazines, deceitful-friendship, vulnerability and even internet-misuse is well explained and tackled accordingly. The definition of virginity in this book got me captivated, Sam O Salau defines “virginity” as a state of remaining chaste and pure by abstaining from all forms of sexually related knowledge of a person's body parts, either with a member of the same sex or the opposite sex.

I can assure you that once you start reading this book you will get to understand that the way to purity is to willfully decide to keep yourself pure by making sexual abstinence your ultimate decision until you are married. Please note that this book does not condemn those who have lost their virginity but it also serves as an eye opener for them to retrace their footsteps and abstain from any sexual sin till marriage.

The writer also negate the ideology and the mentality you might have about the fact that a lot of ladies are getting sexually active by giving examples of well-known celebrity like “Celine Dion”, “Jessica Simpson”, and “Phoebe Kudrow” who remained virgins till they got married. In the most realistic sense “Sex” can only be enjoyed in marriage and not during courtship and it will also interest you to know that teenagers are among the largest and fastest growing group carrying STD's and AID's virus, so therefore I urge you to always remember this fact when you are thinking of giving in to someone's temptation to hand you what could be your death permit.

Please be careful and abstain from sexual sin because your future is far more precious than the sinful pleasure you might get from a night stand. I beseech you to read these book careful and absorb all the content for your personal use and also bless the life of your friends with a copy of this book.

Dcns Ruth Olubisi Ayeni
National President
Foursquare Women International (FWI) Nigeria

What a timely book to be written at a critical time like this. We are in an era whereby things are done upside down. Teenagers are promiscuous let alone youth. The young virtuous ones are made to believe that virginity is a thing of the past and that no one keeps it, neither is it cherished even when kept, this is the deceit of the devil to lure young ones into the world of immorality.

Pastor Sam O Salau has done justice to the book by making everyone who keeps his/her virginity feels exactly good the way God and humanity feels about them. Every child needs it; it is a jewel of preservation of life. The book has saved us from talking too much on the virtue of purity, sanity and self-worth in our annual programmes done for teenagers. I advise that it is a lamp that every home must have laid in the hand of their teenagers to light their path unto a good and Godly living.

Foluso Atilola,
Lead Consultant/Executive Director,
Covenant Educational Consultancy
